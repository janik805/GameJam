# DesertSlayer
DesertSlayer is a fast-paced casual action game for Windows. Battle through the dangerous desert and defeat waves of enemies to claim the highest score.
## The Mission
Survive: Fight off hordes of skeletons or dodge their attacks.
Collect: Grab coins to boost your score.
Compete: Push your limits to reach the ultimate high score.
